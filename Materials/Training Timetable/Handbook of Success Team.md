## **Purpose**

This playbook explains the step-by-step onboarding process for the Onboarding Manager (OM), Onboarding Specialist (OS), and Onboarding Coordinator (OC). It helps everyone stay aligned, follow the same steps, and meet the **Expected Go Live Date (EGLD)**. It also includes special workflows for Big Logo and Complex Setup merchants.  
---

## **Onboarding Team Charter**

### **Team Purpose**

The Onboarding Team is dedicated to empowering and supporting our merchant partners by providing a comprehensive and seamless onboarding experience. Our primary goals are to ensure that merchants are well-prepared to utilize our platform and services, align on expectations, and facilitate their success from day one. This includes guiding merchants through all stages of onboarding, from initial welcome calls to post-go-live support, fostering meaningful relationships, and driving growth within the StoreHub ecosystem.  
---

### **Key Responsibilities**

| Key Responsibilities |  |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Comprehensive Onboarding Process** | Align on all expectations, gather essential onboarding information, and schedule key milestones, including Backoffice and product setup, training, hardware delivery, and installation, ensuring readiness for the merchant's go-live date. |
| **Service Excellence** | Ensure that all onboarding services are delivered to the highest standards and within expected timelines. This includes providing smooth operations on the go-live day and continuing support for the first 30 days to ensure a successful transition. |
| **Merchant Relationship Management** | Build and maintain strong relationships with merchants, addressing their needs and concerns throughout the onboarding process. |
| **Feature Upselling, Implementation, Adoption and Support** | Actively promote add-on features, supplies, and onboarding services by highlighting their value to merchants. Provide hands-on guidance through the setup and adoption of key solutions including POS, Beep Delivery, QR Order & Pay, delivery and payment integrations, loyalty programs, and eCommerce. Offer continued support post-setup to help merchants maximize the impact of these tools. |
| **Continuous Improvement** | Regularly assess and refine onboarding processes to enhance efficiency, effectiveness, and merchant satisfaction. |
