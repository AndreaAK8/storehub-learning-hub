# StoreHub Learning Hub - Coach Guide

## What is the Learning Hub?

A self-paced training platform for new hires that replaces scattered documents, emails, and manual tracking with one unified dashboard.

---

## Objective

Enable trainees to complete 80% of their training independently, freeing coaches to focus on assessments, roleplay, and hands-on guidance.

---

## Key Benefits

| For Trainees | For Coaches |
|--------------|-------------|
| Clear daily schedule - no confusion on what to do next | Less time answering "what should I do now?" |
| Self-paced learning with progress tracking | Real-time visibility into trainee progress |
| Instant access to all materials in one place | Automated reminders and notifications |
| Gamification keeps them engaged | Focus on high-value coaching, not admin |

---

## Features Overview

### 1. My Training (Daily Schedule)

**What it is:** The trainee's home page showing their training schedule organized by day.

**How it works:**
- Displays Day 1, Day 2, Day 3... based on role (OS = 5 days, OC = 4 days, etc.)
- Each day shows activities in order: videos, readings, quizzes, assessments
- Activities include estimated time, success criteria, and a TL;DR summary
- Trainees mark activities as complete when done

**Benefit:** Trainees always know exactly what to do next without asking.

---

### 2. Training Roadmap (Progress Bar)

**What it is:** Visual progress indicator showing which day the trainee is on.

**How it works:**
- Shows all training days as clickable segments
- Current day is highlighted
- Completed days show a checkmark
- Trainees can click to navigate between days

**Benefit:** Gives trainees a sense of progress and accomplishment.

---

### 3. My Scores (Personal Scoreboard)

**What it is:** A dashboard showing the trainee's learning score and assessment results.

**How it works:**
- **Learning Score:** Calculated from weighted assessments (each assessment has a % weight)
- Shows all required assessments with their weights
- Completed assessments show score and contribution to total
- Pending assessments shown as "Not yet completed"
- Expandable breakdown explains exactly how the score is calculated

**Key info for trainees:**
- 80% = passing threshold
- Participation bonus (5-20%) added by trainer at the end
- Score updates automatically as assessments are graded

**Benefit:** Trainees understand exactly where they stand and what they need to pass.

---

### 4. Activity Cards

**What it is:** Individual learning activities within each day.

**How it works:**
- Each card shows: title, type (video/reading/quiz), estimated time
- **Success Criteria:** What the trainee should be able to do after completing
- **TL;DR:** Quick summary of the key takeaways
- Click to access the learning material (Lark links)
- Mark as complete when done

**Benefit:** Trainees know the purpose of each activity, not just "watch this video."

---

### 5. Search (Cmd+K / Ctrl+K)

**What it is:** Global search across all training modules and resources.

**How it works:**
- Press Cmd+K (Mac) or Ctrl+K (Windows) anywhere
- Type to search for topics, modules, or keywords
- Results show matching activities across all days

**Benefit:** Quick access to specific content without scrolling through days.

---

### 6. Help Button

**What it is:** Floating help button for when trainees are stuck.

**How it works:**
- Shows a checklist of self-help steps first (re-read material, check FAQ, etc.)
- If still stuck, provides option to contact assigned coach
- Encourages self-reliance before escalating

**Benefit:** Reduces unnecessary coach interruptions while ensuring support is available.

---

### 7. Onboarding Tour

**What it is:** Guided walkthrough for first-time users.

**How it works:**
- Automatically shows on first login
- 8 steps explaining key features
- Can be dismissed and won't show again

**Benefit:** Trainees understand the platform without needing a live demo.

---

### 8. XP & Gamification

**What it is:** Points system to encourage engagement.

**How it works:**
- Trainees earn XP for completing activities
- Bonus XP for completing faster than estimated time
- Visual celebrations (confetti) on milestones

**Benefit:** Makes training feel rewarding, not like a chore.

---

### 9. Completion Certificate

**What it is:** Downloadable certificate upon training completion.

**How it works:**
- Automatically generated when all days are completed
- Shows trainee name, role, and completion date
- Can be downloaded as image

**Benefit:** Gives trainees a sense of achievement and official recognition.

## What Coaches Need to Do

1. **Grade assessments** - Scores sync automatically to the Learning Hub
2. **Add participation score** - 5-20% based on engagement and attitude
3. **Answer questions** - When trainees use the Help button to escalate
4. **Conduct roleplay/mock sessions** - The hands-on 20% that can't be self-paced
