### DAY 4: Tools Introduction + Welcome Call Script

**Theme:** Know the tools; understand the first merchant touchpoint
**Trainer:** 2 hrs | **Self-Study:** 5.5 hrs | **Coach:** 0.5 hr

| Time | Duration | Topic | Type | Details |
|------|----------|-------|------|---------|
| 9:30-10:30 | 1 hr | Tools Overview | Trainer-Led | Quick tour of Salesforce, Chargebee, Intercom, Aircall, Talkhub (not deep - just where things are) |
| 10:30-11:30 | 1 hr | Hands-On: Find the Merchant | Exercise | Given 3 real merchant emails, find them in SF + Chargebee; document what you find |
| 11:30-12:00 | 30 min | Tools Checkpoint | Coach Check | Player explains findings; coach validates understanding |
| 12:00-1:00 | 1 hr | Lunch | - | - |
| 1:00-2:00 | 1 hr | Welcome Call Script Study | Self-Study | Read script; annotate key questions and data points |
| 2:00-3:00 | 1 hr | Real Recording Analysis | Exercise | Listen to 2 actual Welcome Call recordings; complete analysis worksheet |
| 3:00-3:30 | 30 min | Welcome Call Walkthrough | Trainer-Led | Script highlights; critical moments; common mistakes to avoid |
| 3:30-5:00 | 1.5 hr | Welcome Call Practice #1 | Self-Record | Self-record Welcome Call using real merchant scenario; upload to Lark |
| 5:00-6:00 | 1 hr | Self-Review + Reflection | Self-Study | Watch your own recording; note improvement areas |

**Day 4 Outcomes:**
- Know where to find info in tools
- Understand Welcome Call flow
- 1 self-recorded practice submitted

### WEEK 2 DAYS 3-5: Buddying (Simple Accounts)

**Definition:** "You do the work, I'll be there to answer questions"

| Day | Accounts | Focus | Support Level |
|-----|----------|-------|---------------|
| Day 3 | 3-4 simple accounts | Full onboarding workflow | Senior MOM available nearby |
| Day 4 | 3-4 simple accounts | Increasing independence | Senior MOM spot-checks |
| Day 5 | 3-4 simple accounts | Near-independent | Senior MOM reviews at EOD |

**Simple Account Definition:**
- No hardware chasers
- 0-2 required features
- No complex setups

---

### WEEK 2 END: Assessment & Feedback (1 hr with TL)

| Focus | Method |
|-------|--------|
| Progress check | Review accounts handled; identify gaps |
| Understanding check | Ask player to explain onboarding process end-to-end |
| Sentiment check | Gauge confidence, comfort with job/team |
| Next steps | Set Week 3 goals |
